About

Welcome to my website! My name is Supakorn Juengsophonvitavas but I normally go by Nikko. I am second year Astrophysics student at the University of St Andrews. I am mainly interested in the evolution of of high mass stars, especially in the late stages of their lives. I have worked briefly with National Astronomical Institution of Thailand mainly as a public outreach intern and shadowed the researchers at the institution. In first year, I was the Student representative for both the first year Astronomy module and the Astronomical Society. For the full list of what I have done, make sure to check out my experience and how to contact me.

Apart from working on Astrophysics, I am also keen on performing magic as a form of performance art. I have travelled the world and attend numerous Magic conventions, as well as some competition, imagine the Olympics but for Magic! I normally perform card magic but I do have some parlour tricks up my sleeves and I would be excited to share them with you

Tutoring

I am currently tutoring for mainly high school students in both Mathematics and Physics at IGCSE or A-Levels, as well as Astronomy GCSE. I focus on the understanding of the content, and the exam technique rather than memorising the answer to the markscheme. As part of service, I am also happy to provide basic advices for those who are interested in applying to University both in the USA and UK.

Qualification: 3 A* (Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry) and 1 (Further Mathematics) A at A-levels. I am open to teach other for other qualification with overlapping content, however, I may not be able to provide the same level of guidance.
